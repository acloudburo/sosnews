    A bar built in an old cowshed brings Alpine Music to the old town.

    coffee&bar

    Posted on Nov 15

    The Älpli Bar brings Swiss folklore to the center of Zurich. A Swiss style accordion, a contrabass and a washboard stand ready for musicians all the time - and more often than you‘d think someone stops by and plays some Alpine folk music. it might not be the hippest place but very traditional, very Swiss and it has been around for age. Try the rather strong trademark drink called the Älpli Milch.
