Musée Visionnaire just a short walk from the Townhouse and the Plattenhof

art

Posted on Nov 15


Be visionary - be welcome The new museum is an art foyer & and art-lending library (Artothek) operated by the Musée Visionnaire, a non-profit association. Changing exhibitions will allow visitors to discover and re-discover contemporary Art Brut | Outsider Art and experience it within the context of related art forms. Special social events will be dedicated to topical subjects touching upon the world of art and current public issues.
