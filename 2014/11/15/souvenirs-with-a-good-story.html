    Souvenirs with a good story

    shopping

    Posted on Nov 15

    When I travel somewhere, I either buy a souvenir because the sales clerk is so lovely or because something has it’s own story. Here in Zurich you’ll find both at Changemaker. And the stories their products tell you are even good, about how organic, eco-friendly, fair & social or handmade they are. This makes your souvenir for your friend more meaningful and special, and not to forget, they are all really cool. And don’t forget to buy something for yourself…
    Changemaker Marktgasse 10 8001 Zürich
