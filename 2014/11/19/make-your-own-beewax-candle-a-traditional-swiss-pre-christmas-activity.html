Make your own beewax candle, a traditional Swiss pre-Christmas activity

leisure

Posted on Nov 19


When Kerzenziehen (candle making) starts at Bürkliplatz I know Christmas is just around the corner. In the pavillion on Bürkliplatz you  can make your own candle by dipping a wick into tubs of hot, molten wax.  You need is a bit of patience, as the wax needs to cool before you dip it again. Good to slow down for a while in the frantic pre-Christmas period. All the proceeds go to charity, you pay according to weight.
 Open daily from 10.00am until 8.00pm
