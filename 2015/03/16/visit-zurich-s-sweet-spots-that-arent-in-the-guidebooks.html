    Visit Zürich's sweet spots that aren’t in the guidebooks

    coffee&bar

    Posted on Mar 16

    An original fun and delicious way to visit our city, and learn that it’s not all about milk chocolate in the Alps. Kerrin of MyKugelhopf, true connoisseur of desserts, confections and chocolate, will let her sweet tooth guide you around the charming old town to discover some of the city’s most irresistible sweets .
    Sweet Zürich” tours are Tuesday to Friday afternoons at 2pm.
    Tours stay small (2-8 people), last about 2.5 hours and cost CHF 85 per person. All you need are comfortable shoes and a love of sweets! 
    http://mykugelhopf.ch/sweet-zurich-tour/
