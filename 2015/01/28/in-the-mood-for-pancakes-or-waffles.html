In the mood for pancakes or waffles

coffee&bar

Posted on Jan 28

Zum Guten Gluck, is a bright coffee shop with a modern Scandinavian touch that serves delicious pancakes all day long. Their pancake creations range from sweet to savory, my favourite being the one with ricotta. They also serve a very good breakfast 7 days a week.
