    Zukunft - a club dedicated to music with local and international DJ's and bands

    coffee&bar

    Posted on Jan 28

    Zukunft is a rather  small club in the trendy Kreis 4 : a good place for  electro, house and crossover party styles. The entrance is not easy to find, hidden in passage next to Bar 3000. It’s not your see and to be seen club but  rather a place where good music and drinks are the main attraction. Hipsters and Rockers  party until the break of dawn. Open Thursday trough Saturday as of 11.00 pm. http://www.zukunft.cl/
