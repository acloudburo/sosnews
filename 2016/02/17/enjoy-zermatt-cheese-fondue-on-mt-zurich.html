    Enjoy Zermatt Cheese Fondue on Mt. Zurich

    restaurant

    Posted on Feb 17

    Until the 20th of March, the Alpine romance of Zermatt comes to Mt. Zurich.

    The charming wooden hut, which used to be the post office in Zermatt, serves authentic Valais Cheese Fondue in a very cozy alpine setting.

    Baracca at the Zürichberg: open Monday - Sunday 5.30pm to 11.00pm
