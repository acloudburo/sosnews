    LULULEMON is now open in Zurich

    shopping

    Posted on Feb 17

    Lululemon who makes yoga-inspired athletic clothing, has recently opened a store in the old town of Zurich. At their store they not only sell great looking athletic clothing but also host public events.

    Every Thursday it is Run club from 6.30 until 7.30 pm or on Fridays from 6.00 until 7.00 pm they offer free yoga classes: first come, first mat. (ask at the Front desk of the Plattenhof or Townhouse to lend you a Yoga mat)
